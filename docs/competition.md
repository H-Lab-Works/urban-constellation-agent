# Competition Notes

Project name: Urban Constellation

Competition result: third prize in the China Graduate Smart City Technology and Creative Design Competition.

Personal certificates and official proof files are intentionally excluded from this portfolio repository to avoid exposing private information. They can be provided separately when needed.

## Submitted Concept

Urban Constellation combines migration data, urban network analysis, AI Agent workflows, and visual decision support for planning, transportation, regional coordination, and public-service allocation scenarios.

## Key Scenarios

- National migration-flow monitoring.
- City-pair flow prediction.
- Policy net-effect evaluation.
- Future planning scenario simulation.
- Hubei urban hierarchy and community-structure analysis.
